# 🎮 The Last Dev: Syntharia's Awakening

> **The Last Dev** is a narrative-driven RPG educational experience where code is magic and logic is survival.

---

## 🌌 The Concept
In the fading realm of **Syntharia**, the ancient knowledge of "The Code" has been lost to time, and the world's light is dimming. You are the **Last Dev**, the only one capable of restoring the realm's core by solving logical barriers woven into the fabric of reality.

Guided by **Oryn**, an ancient AI sentinel, you must navigate through treacherous landscapes, manage your resources, and prove your mastery over the fundamental building blocks of magic.

---

## ⚔️ Character Classes
Each class starts with a unique, one-time-use **Arcane Artifact**:

- **🛡️ Warrior (Galahed)**: Starts with the *Stone Shield* (Absorbs 1 failure).
- **🧙 Mage (Kael)**: Starts with the *Sword of Omission* (Skips 1 challenge).
- **🏹 Archer (Artemis)**: Starts with the *Arcane Vision* (Reveals the truth).

---

## 🗺️ The Path to the Kernel
Your journey spans five distinct regions of Syntharia:

1.  **🌲 Forest of First Spells**: Where initiates learn to weave.
2.  **🏔️ Mountains of Lost Code**: High-altitude logic puzzles.
3.  **🏚️ Village of the Broken Compiler**: Fixing the stability of society.
4.  **🌋 Volcano of Eternal Bugs**: The heat of debugging under pressure.
5.  **🏰 Castle of the Dark Kernel**: The final restoration at the heart of the world.

---

## 🧠 The Oryn Agent
**Oryn** is not just an NPC; he is a dynamic agent integrated via the Model Context Protocol (MCP).
- **Adaptive Narrative**: Reacts to your class, performance, and current zone.
- **Session Memory**: Remembers your previous triumphs and struggles.
- **Dynamic Challenges**: Uses the `get_challenge()` MCP tool to scout for appropriate trials based on your progress.

---

## 🛒 Economy & Survival
- **Life (5 Integrity Units)**: Failure cost units. Reaching zero ends the session.
- **Currency (Gold Chips)**: Earned through correct logic implementation and streaks.
- **The Forge (Shop)**: Use chips to acquire more artifacts (Shields, Skips, Visions, and more).
- **Quick Belt (Inventory)**: Up to 3 artifacts ready for immediate use during challenges.

---

## 🚀 Vision
*The Last Dev* aims to merge the thrill of classical RPG exploration with the modern frontier of AI-driven gaming, serving as a showcase for **agentic architecture** and **LLM integration**.
